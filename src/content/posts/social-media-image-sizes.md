Using correctly sized images on social media ensures your content looks professional and gets maximum engagement. Each platform has specific requirements.

## Instagram

Square posts work best at 1080x1080 pixels. Portrait posts at 1080x1350 get more screen real estate in feeds. Stories and Reels require 1080x1920 (9:16 ratio). Profile pictures display at 320x320 but upload at least 400x400.

## Facebook

Post images display best at 1200x630. Cover photos need 820x312 pixels (but upload 820x462 for mobile display). Event covers use 1200x628. Stories match Instagram at 1080x1920.

## Twitter / X

In-feed images display best at 1200x675 (16:9 ratio). Header photos need 1500x500 pixels. Profile pictures display at 400x400.

## LinkedIn

Post images work best at 1200x627. Company page banners need 1128x191. Profile photos display at 400x400. Article cover images use 1200x644.

## YouTube

Thumbnails must be 1280x720 (16:9). Channel banners need 2560x1440 for all devices. Profile pictures display at 800x800.

## TikTok and Pinterest

TikTok videos use 1080x1920 (9:16). Pinterest pins perform best at 1000x1500 (2:3 ratio). Use our Social Image Resizer to quickly resize images for any platform.
