Instagram, TikTok, and other platforms limit you to one clickable link in your profile. Link-in-bio tools solve this by creating a landing page with multiple links.

## When You Need One

If you regularly promote different content, products, or pages, a link-in-bio page is essential. It is especially useful for creators, small businesses, and anyone with multiple online presences.

## Free vs Paid Options

Paid tools like Linktree, Beacons, and Stan Store offer analytics, customization, and integrations. But a simple HTML page works perfectly for most people. Our Link in Bio Page tool creates one instantly for free.

## What to Include

Prioritize your most important link at the top. Include your website or store, latest content, lead magnet or freebie, and social profiles. Keep it to 5-7 links maximum. Too many options leads to decision paralysis.

## Design Tips

Match your bio page colors to your brand. Use clear, action-oriented link titles. Include your name and a brief description at the top. Keep it mobile-friendly since most visitors come from mobile.

## Performance Tips

Rotate your top link based on current promotions. Add new links for time-sensitive content. Remove links that get no clicks. If using a paid tool, check analytics regularly to optimize placement.
